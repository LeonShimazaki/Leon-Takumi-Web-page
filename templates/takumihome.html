About Japanese taiko group TAKUMI is a Christchurch formed entertainment group performing at functions/schools and cultural events hoping to spread Japanese culture through heart pounding beats and stunning visual performance
Biography TAKUMI formed in 2008, by Mr. Tomo Mameno. Formerly the principal of Japanese Supplementary School of Christchurch Canterbury. Wishing for the Japanese Society to "spread the uniqueness, the power and beauty that Japanese traditional music holds" Starting with only a few members, we have now expanded to a group of roughly 50 members. As the years went by, local schools and associations started to contact us and offered us numerous opportunities to perform regularly. We have had the privilege to perform several times at the annual Santa Parade held every Summer, Culture Galore of CPIT, Christmas in the Park 2012, Community events held across the city and several schools from Primary to High Schools. We must thank all those who have attended our concerts so far and to those who have enjoyed our performance, thank you for your support! We will strive to develop ourselves individually, but also as a performance group interacting with the community to spread out culture and hopefully spread some extra spice into the music events of Christchurch. Arigato gozaimasu! Thank you!
